Threads

There are a variety of threads you can use when doing embroidery, the most common being cotton which comes in 6 strands. This can come in more than 500 different colours from single colours to threads with blended colours. These can be found in any craft shop with the most common brands being Anchor and DMC. Other popular threads include Metallic and Silk which are great for a strong and shiny finish to your work! They go particularly well with a contrasting fabric such as linen or evenweave.
